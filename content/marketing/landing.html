Who Camp Claude is for

If Claude is showing up at your job, this is your edge.

Most Claude training starts too late. It assumes you already know what an LLM is, how agents differ from chatbots, and which surface to use. Camp Claude starts at the ground floor and walks you all the way to real, working automations — built around the actual tools you already use every day.

Marketers

Financial Analysts

Not for you if: you already live in the terminal, work comfortably with Claude Code, or write production code for a living. This isn't a developer program. It's for the 95% of Claude users who don't code.

Common questions

What campers usually ask.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. Camp Claude is specifically for non-developers. If you're comfortable installing software and opening folders on your computer, you have everything you need. Campers come from marketing, operations, design, HR, legal, founding teams, and every other non-technical role.

I already use Claude a little — is this still for me?

Yes, and honestly that's the ideal starting point. Most campers come in having used Claude as a chat assistant for a few months and feel like they're leaving value on the table. Camp Claude is the structured jump from "I ask Claude questions" to "Claude runs parts of my job on its own." If you already know the basics, you'll move faster through the early sessions and get to the automation payoff sooner.

What kinds of automations will I actually build?

Real ones, tailored to your work. Marketers build campaign and content automations with tools like Canva and Notion. Financial analysts build skills that auto-generate Excel reports. Consultants turn notes and interviews into structured deliverables. Ops teams automate recurring reports and document reviews. You'll leave Session 5 with something running in your own account that you actually use.

What do I need before Session 1?

A laptop, a Claude account (the app is free to install), and one real task you want Claude to take off your plate. We'll walk you through installing the desktop app in the pre-camp videos, and you'll arrive at Session 1 with everything already set up.

How much time should I expect to spend?

Roughly 10–12 hours total across the program — about 30 minutes for the pre-camp videos, then five 90-minute live sessions, plus some light between-session practice. Most campers finish in about three weeks.

What if I miss a session?

Every session is recorded and posted in the Campfire community within a day. You can catch up async and bring your questions to the next live session or the community space between sessions.

Who's running the camp?

Camp Claude is a RUDI program — Responsible Use of Digital Intelligence. RUDI is a training company focused on helping non-technical people adopt AI tools intentionally, with strong mental models and real working skills. Camp Claude is our flagship program.

Can my company send a team?

Yes — either send individuals to the Open Cohort, or book a Private Cohort where we deliver the whole program to your team with curriculum customized to your tools. Private cohorts are flat-fee engagements and can be scheduled around your team's calendar.
